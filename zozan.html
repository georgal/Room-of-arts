Artists of the Small Room Project

Zozan Hamo

My name is Zozan Hamo, born in 1967, in Afrin of Syria.

I have four children.

I graduated from the Institute of Women's Arts.

I received a diploma in educational qualification and several certificates of attendance with the MEDA project of the European Union.

I spent a lot of time on drawing because of work, marriage and children for nearly 16 years. Throughout this period, my long-delayed dream did not stop.

When I arrived in Greece, the hardships of life we ​​lived in and thousands of people was very painful. I could not find a way to express the painful reality in words.

Colors took their chance and brought the true image of the suffering and the difficulties facing each moment. I am expressing through of my paintings for humanity, justice and peace.
