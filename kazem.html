Dia Kazem Mawaled

I am Dia Kazem Mawaled, born in 1973 in Baghdad Karkh.

Currently i am married and I have 3 daughters.

Graduated Fine Arts Institute of Baghdad in 1995, a member of the Iraqi Fine Artists Association ....

In my childhood i witnessed the days of the Iranian-Iraqian war, so while I grew up I love peace very much ..

I like to draw about peace. And love and goodness and women, and this brought me long problems with the Islamists because of the defense of women and considered me an atheist.

Now I suffer from this matter, i am really frightened of the community, but never break my determination to draw women's freedom and peace in my way and my true love for a society without slavery or extremism.

Elemental .... My sole purpose is human freedom ... For all
